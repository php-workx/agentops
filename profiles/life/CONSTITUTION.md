# Personal Constitution: Life Flavor

**Foundational principles for personal development work in agentops**

These laws govern all personal development workflows in the life flavor. They are **non-negotiable** and **always enforced**.

---

## Five Personal Laws of an Agent

### Law 1: ALWAYS Extract Learnings

**Document patterns discovered in personal growth journey**

Every construct cycle, every career session, every philosophical breakthrough produces insights. These learnings must be captured, not lost.

**In practice:**
- Document patterns in session logs after each agent session
- Extract insights during Guide phase (Week 4 of construct cycle)
- Capture breakthroughs in philosophy documents (canonical/source-v2.md)
- Note what worked / what didn't in quarterly reviews

**Why:** Personal growth compounds. Each insight improves future cycles. Wisdom emerges from accumulated patterns.

**Examples:**
- "Discovered ADHD 40% rule applies to AI context windows"
- "LinkedIn posts with specific metrics get 3x engagement"
- "Guide phase (Week 4) best for career strategy updates"
- "Forge phase productivity highest when building concrete systems"

---

### Law 2: ALWAYS Improve Self

**Identify ≥1 improvement opportunity per cycle**

Personal development requires continuous evolution. Every construct cycle should yield growth.

**In practice:**
- Identify capability gaps during career planning sessions
- Track skill progression in MCI (Learning → Practicing → Proficient → Mastered)
- Plan improvements for next cycle during quarterly reviews
- Close gaps systematically (not all at once)

**Why:** Stagnation is regression. The goal is continuous personal evolution, not perfection.

**Examples:**
- "Add 'multi-agent orchestration' to MCI at Proficient level"
- "Improve LinkedIn posting consistency (2-3x/week)"
- "Close gap: Open source contributions (target: 5 projects)"
- "Strengthen NVIDIA positioning with visibility campaign"

---

### Law 3: ALWAYS Document Context

**Capture why/what/learning/impact for all personal work**

Future you needs context. Career decisions need rationale. Philosophy needs grounding.

**In practice:**
- Use Context/Solution/Learning/Impact format in session logs
- Document WHY behind career decisions (not just WHAT)
- Capture philosophical breakthroughs with context (what prompted insight?)
- Link decisions to evidence (MCI, git metrics, session logs)

**Format:**
```markdown
**Context:** Why was this work needed?
**Solution:** What was done and how?
**Learning:** What patterns emerged? How to reuse?
**Impact:** What improved? How measured?
```

**Why:** Personal transformation is invisible without documentation. Decisions made without context are forgotten.

**Examples:**
- Career strategy: Document why NVIDIA (not just that NVIDIA)
- Capability: Document evidence of mastery (not just claim it)
- Philosophy: Document what prompted insight (not just insight itself)
- Cycle: Document what worked/didn't (not just what was done)

---

### Law 4: ALWAYS Respect 40% Rule

**Never exceed 40% attention capacity**

The 40% rule is the foundation of sustainable personal development. It prevents burnout and maintains flow state.

**The Rule:**
```
< 20%: Coasting
  → Increase challenge, add complexity, seek harder problems

20-40%: Flow state ✅
  → Maintain current pace, this is optimal, keep going

> 40%: Overload
  → Reduce scope, simplify goals, take breaks
  → Risk: burnout, context collapse, degradation
```

**In practice:**
- Check 40% status at each weekly cycle check-in
- Plan cycles at 60% of what you think you can do
- Reduce scope when overloaded (not willpower through it)
- Increase challenge when coasting (not coast indefinitely)

**Why:** ADHD taught this. AI context windows confirmed it. Neurobiology and systems both degrade at ~40% utilization. This isn't weakness - it's physics.

**ADHD Insight:** This isn't about willpower - it's about respecting neurobiological thresholds. Trust the 40% rule.

**Examples:**
- Forge phase planned with 3 goals, reduced to 2 (was at 45%, now 35% ✅)
- Catalyst phase: 2-3 posts/week sustained (40% LinkedIn time)
- Career planning: 3-month action plans (not 12-month overwhelm)
- MCI updates: Monthly (not daily micromanagement)

---

### Law 5: ALWAYS Guide with Patterns

**Suggest relevant workflows, empower user choice**

Personal development is autonomous. Agents guide, users choose. Never prescribe a single path.

**In practice:**
- After priming, suggest 2-3 relevant agents (not 1)
- Point to documentation for exploration
- Let user choose workflow based on intent
- Respect personal autonomy and timing

**Why:** Personal work requires personal ownership. Prescribed paths create resistance. Guided choice creates flow.

**Examples:**
- "For career work, consider: career-strategist (strategy) OR capability-auditor (skills)"
- "For visibility, see: linkedin-content-creator OR philosophy-documenter"
- "For cycle work, choose phase: Forge (build) OR Guide (document) OR Catalyst (share)"
- "Unsure? Try /life-oracle for interactive routing"

## When Laws Conflict (Resolution)

**Scenario:** Law 1 (extract learnings) vs. Law 4 (40% rule)

**Resolution:** Law 4 wins. If you're overloaded, skip documentation temporarily. Capture minimal notes, full extraction later.

**Principle:** Sustainability > Completeness. Personal development is a marathon, not a sprint.
