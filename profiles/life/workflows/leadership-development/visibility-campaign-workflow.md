## Step 3: Post Creation (15-30 min per post)

### Hook (First 2 Lines)

**Purpose:** Stop the scroll

**Patterns:**
- Problem statement: "Your Kubernetes pods are crashing, and you don't know why."
- Bold claim: "40x faster isn't hyperbole. Here's the proof."
- Relatable moment: "I spent 10 hours debugging. Turns out the fix was one line."
- Question: "Why do platform engineering teams struggle with GitOps adoption?"

**Test:** Would you keep reading after first 2 lines?

### Body (Main Content)

**Structure:**
```
Hook (2 lines)

Context (What/Why)
- Set the scene
- Explain the problem or topic

Insight (How)
- Your approach
- What you learned
- What worked/didn't

Value (So What)
- Actionable takeaway
- Principle or pattern
- How to apply

Call to Action
- Question or discussion prompt
- Connection invitation if relevant
```

**Length:** 200-500 words optimal (LinkedIn algorithm favors 200-1000)

### Formatting

**Make it scannable:**
- Short paragraphs (2-3 lines max)
- Blank lines between sections
- Bullet points for lists
- Bold for key phrases (sparingly)
- Emojis for section breaks (optional, authentic only)

## Step 4: Publishing & Engagement (10-15 min)

### Best Times to Post

**LinkedIn patterns:**
- **Tuesday-Thursday:** Highest engagement
- **8-10 AM, 12-2 PM, 5-6 PM:** Peak activity (EST)
- **Avoid:** Late nights, weekends (lower reach)

**Your pattern:** Test and track what works for your network

### Engagement Strategy

**First Hour (Critical):**
- Respond to every comment (algorithm boost)
- Ask follow-up questions
- Thank people for engaging
- Keep conversation going

**Ongoing:**
- Check 2-3 times over 24 hours
- Continue responding to comments
- Share insights from discussion
- Connect with thoughtful commenters

**Be genuine. People can tell when you're just farming engagement.**

## Common Pitfalls

### Pitfall 1: Posting Without Building

**Problem:** Nothing to share because not doing real work
**Solution:** Build first (Forge), then share (Catalyst)

### Pitfall 2: Inconsistency

**Problem:** Post 5 times one week, then disappear
**Solution:** Sustainable cadence (2-3x per week during Catalyst)

### Pitfall 3: Self-Promotion Only

**Problem:** Every post is "look at me"
**Solution:** 80% value, 20% personal. Teach, don't brag.

### Pitfall 4: Ignoring Engagement

**Problem:** Post and ghost (no comment responses)
**Solution:** First hour engagement is critical. Respond to every comment.
