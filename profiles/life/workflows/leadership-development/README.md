## The Leadership Pattern

```
Build (Forge) → Document (Guide) → Teach (Catalyst) → Scale (Resonance)
```

**Core insight:** You can't teach what you haven't built. You shouldn't build without teaching.

---

## Integration with Construct Cycle

**Forge phase (Weeks 1-3):** Build proof
- Technical work creates credibility
- Deliverables become case studies
- Problems solved become teaching topics

**Guide phase (Week 4):** Document insights
- Extract patterns worth sharing
- Prepare teaching materials
- Clarify thinking for explanation

**Catalyst phase (Week 5):** Share and teach
- 2-3 LinkedIn posts published
- Mentorship or teaching sessions
- Professional visibility built

**Resonance phase (Week 6):** Measure impact
- Engagement tracked
- Influence assessed
- Next visibility priorities identified

---

## Success Metrics

**Weekly (Catalyst phase):**
- ✅ 2-3 visibility actions (posts, comments, shares)
- ✅ Engagement measured (reactions, comments, connections)

**Monthly:**
- ✅ 8-12 LinkedIn posts or interactions
- ✅ Growing connection network (target: +20/month)
- ✅ Consistent professional presence

**Quarterly:**
- ✅ Visible expertise in platform engineering
- ✅ Active engagement with community
- ✅ Teaching or mentorship sessions delivered
- ✅ Professional recognition growing

**Annually:**
- ✅ 500+ meaningful connections
- ✅ Established thought leadership
- ✅ Speaking or teaching opportunities
- ✅ Professional brand aligned with career goals
