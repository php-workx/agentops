### Quick Tip

**Structure:**
```
Hook: [Quick problem statement]

Tip: [Actionable advice]

Why it works: [Brief explanation]

CTA: [Try it or share yours]
```

**Example:**
```
Debugging Kubernetes? Check these in order:

1. Pods (kubectl get pods)
2. Logs (kubectl logs)
3. Events (kubectl get events)
4. Describe (kubectl describe)
5. YAML (kubectl get -o yaml)

This order catches 90% of issues quickly.

What's your debugging checklist?
```
