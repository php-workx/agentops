## Step 2: Draft Post Content

**LinkedIn post structure:**

```markdown
## [Engaging Hook - One Line]

[2-3 paragraphs explaining concept/story]

[Key insight or revelation]

[Proof point or evidence]

[Call to action or question for engagement]

---

Hashtags: #AI #DevOps #GitOps #PlatformEngineering #AgentOps
```

**Best practices:**
- Start with hook (grab attention)
- Tell story (engage emotionally)
- Provide value (insight, how-to, pattern)
- Include proof (metrics, evidence)
- End with engagement (question, CTA)
- Use hashtags strategically (5-7 max)

**Mistake:** Inconsistent posting (1 post then silent for month)
**Fix:** 2-3 posts per week during campaign, then maintain rhythm

**Mistake:** Writing for everyone
**Fix:** Write for NVIDIA hiring managers - what do THEY care about?
